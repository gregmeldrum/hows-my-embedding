How do you know if your embedding model is producing good results? What if you could visualize your embeddings to see structure in terms of density and clustering. Here are some examples of text embedding visualized with a 3 dimensional projection. The embeddings are from different models on a the same set of html documents.

As with any dimensional reduction projection, the visualization is not perfect and some information is lost and/or distorted. However, it can give you a good idea of the structure of the embeddings.

The plots below are interactive so feel free to zoom in and out and rotate to explore the structure of the embeddings.
